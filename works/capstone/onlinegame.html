Online Game Platform

←

This is an online game platform where you can play various games with other players. It's a multiplayer platform that allows you to compete with other players in real-time. I made this whole website for a Chinese studio that develops online games. The studio wanted a platform where players can play their games online without downloading them.

The platform is built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It uses WebSocket to establish a connection between the client and the server.

There are many games available on the platform, which are basically original games developed by the studio. The games are simple and fun, and they are designed to be played in a short amount of time. The platform also has a chat feature that allows players to communicate with each other while playing.

The platform is still in development, and I'm working on adding more features and games to it.
